Overview

The Odoo Apps for Customer/Vendor Accounting Statements and Overdue Payments is a robust tool that empowers businesses to efficiently manage financial transactions with their customers and vendors. This feature-rich application provides a comprehensive set of functionalities, allowing users to generate detailed statements, monitor overdue payments, and facilitate seamless communication. With customizable date range filtering, users can tailor statements to specific time frames, including an opening balance for a holistic view of financial positions. The app's automation features enable the automatic sending of statements via email, ensuring regular communication and transparency in financial dealings. The inclusion of total due and overdue amounts in the Statements tab enhances visibility into outstanding balances. Additionally, the option to grant portal access to customers and suppliers promotes self-service, enabling them to independently review their statements. Overall, this Odoo app contributes to a more streamlined and transparent financial workflow, providing businesses with a powerful tool for effective financial management.

Configuration

No additional configuration required

Features

  • Users can easily access and view detailed accounting statements for both customers and suppliers.
  • Statements are presented with due range tables, such as 0-30 days, 30-60 days, 60-90 days, and 90+ days, offering a clear overview of outstanding payments.
  • The app facilitates the seamless distribution of account statements via email to customers and suppliers.
  • Email automation allows for the regular sending of statements, promoting timely communication and transparency in financial dealings.
  • Users can generate reports specifically highlighting overdue payments, aiding in the identification of outstanding amounts and facilitating efficient follow-up.
  • Accountants have the flexibility to filter statements based on start and end dates, providing a customizable view of financial transactions within a specified time frame.
  • Automate the process of sending customer and vendor statements, streamlining communication and ensuring regular updates without manual intervention.
